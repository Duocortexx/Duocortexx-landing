At Duocortex, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our medical education platform.

Information We Collect

  • Personal details you provide during registration (name, email, medical credentials).
  • Activity data such as quiz participation, forum posts, and peer matches.
  • Device and usage information to improve app performance.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide personalized matches, notifications, quizzes, and forum features.
  • To improve app functionality and user experience.
  • To communicate important updates and announcements.

Sharing and Security

  • Your data is shared only with verified users for community interactions.
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Your Choices

  • You can update or delete your personal information anytime.
  • You can manage notification preferences within the app.

Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies or similar technologies to enhance app performance and analyze usage.

Changes to This Policy

Duocortex may update this policy periodically. Users will be notified of major changes.
