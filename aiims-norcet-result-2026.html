AIIMS NORCET 10 Result 2026 Overview

The All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) has officially released the NORCET 10 Prelims Result on 16th April 2026. This recruitment drive aims to fill 2,779 Nursing Officer positions across various AIIMS institutions in India, revised upward from the initially advertised 2,551 vacancies.

The Prelims examination (Stage-I) was conducted on 11th April 2026, and the result has been published within just five days. Candidates who have qualified in the Prelims will now proceed to the Mains examination scheduled for 30th April 2026. The result is available as a downloadable PDF on the official AIIMS examinations portal.

How to Check AIIMS NORCET 10 Result 2026

Follow these steps to download and check your NORCET 10 Prelims result:

  1. Visit the official AIIMS examination portal at aiimsexams.ac.in
  2. Navigate to the "Result" or "Important Notices" section on the homepage
  3. Look for the link titled "NORCET-10 Stage-I Result 2026" and click on it
  4. The result PDF will open in your browser or begin downloading automatically
  5. Press Ctrl+F (or Cmd+F on Mac) and search for your roll number in the PDF
  6. Verify your details including roll number, rank, category, and qualification status
  7. Save or print the result document for your records and future reference

Direct Link: Visit aiimsexams.ac.in and check the Results section to download the NORCET 10 Prelims Result PDF.

AIIMS NORCET 10 Merit List 2026

AIIMS prepares the Prelims merit list based on each candidate's performance in the online examination. The merit list PDF includes the roll numbers of all qualified candidates along with essential details such as:

  • Roll Number of the candidate
  • Category (General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS, PwBD)
  • Gender of the candidate
  • Percentile Score obtained in the Prelims
  • Rank secured in the examination

It is important to note that AIIMS does not issue separate Prelims scorecards with subject-wise marks. Once the result PDF is uploaded on the official website, candidates can directly download it and search for their roll number. The merit list serves as the qualifying document for progression to the Mains stage.

AIIMS NORCET 10 Cut Off Percentile 2026

The cut off percentile determines the minimum score a candidate needs to qualify for the Mains examination. Below are the expected category-wise cut off percentiles based on previous NORCET trends and the current vacancy count:

NORCET 10 Selection Process 2026

The AIIMS Nursing Officer recruitment through NORCET follows a three-stage selection process:

Stage 1: Prelims (Screening Examination)

The Prelims stage is a screening test designed to shortlist candidates for the Mains examination. It was conducted on 11th April 2026, and the results have now been declared. Only candidates who meet the category-wise cut off percentile qualify for the next stage.

Stage 2: Mains Examination

The Mains examination, scheduled for 30th April 2026, evaluates candidates on advanced nursing knowledge, clinical decision-making skills, and applied medical concepts. Performance in Mains determines the final merit ranking for appointment to Nursing Officer positions.

Stage 3: Document Verification

Candidates who qualify through the Mains exam will be called for document verification. This stage involves authentication of educational qualifications, category certificates, identity documents, and other relevant credentials.
